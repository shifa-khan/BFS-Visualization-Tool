BFS Pseudocode
BFS(G, start_node):
    1. Create a queue Q
    2. Mark start_node as visited and enqueue it to Q
    3. While Q is not empty:
        a. Dequeue vertex u from Q
        b. For each neighbor v of u:
            If v is not visited:
                i. Mark v as visited
                ii. Enqueue v to Q
